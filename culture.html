Meet the people

Discover the characters that give Web world it's distinct personality.

HTML, otherwise known as Hypertext Markup Language, is the structure of every region in Web-World. All the pieces, or content, of web world can be found in HTML. It is not much to look at it but it is robust, to the point and really has everything you will need to make up a region of web-world.

*Not all characters are represented in the table below.

Common Characters Name Traits Location
Comments Comments Keeps everyone informed. As needed to explain regions to makers and travelers of web-world.
Angle Brackets Goes around all the regions of web-world. Around everything!
! Exclamation point Excitable and always the first to show up. Found at the top of every web-page in the doctype.
/ Forward Slash Makes sure everything is properly contained...if absent chaos ensues. Found at the end of the line always making sure everything is moving forward.
a links A very social character keeping everyone in web-world connected. links are constructed and thus found anywhere two points that might not otherwise to connected, just like a bridge.
